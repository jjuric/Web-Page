Who are we?

Relaxed gaming is a community of gamers that love unwinding from life with games

Whether it is board games, console or PC games, we love playing them. If you want to see our gameplay, hear our thoughts about games or simply just relax and talk to like minded people, join the community and follow our stream. Oh, and sometimes we also like to talk about programming/developing!

The Founders

Jakov Jurić

Jakov is one of the streamers and the guy whose idea was to start Relaxed Gaming. With the help of his friends/co-founders they created the brand and the rest is history. He is not only a gamer but also a mobile developer and full time student.

Elon Musk

Elon is a full-time streamer and he streams the most out of the original founders. He doesn't do much else besides game and talk about some crazy ideas about space. Nonetheless, he is a fun, cool guy with a wild imagination.

Ante Bartulović

Ante is the manager of Relaxed Gaming, successful student and Android developer. He is proficient in economics so he also takes care of our taxes. He/s rarely on stream, but when he drops by laughter is ensured.
